Flight Booking FAQs

How do I book a flight?

In order to book flight ticket logon, enter departure and arrival destination, travel dates, number of passengers and travel class to get a list of all available flights that you can compare and choose from.

Can I change my flight booking?

You can change the time or date of your booking but change fee and fare difference may apply on top of the price you have paid in your purchase earlier.

How do I cancel my flight?

Wish to cancel your booking? Enter your PNR/Booking reference number, mention your email ID/Last name and proceed for cancellation.
